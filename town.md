# Town QoL
---
This mod modifies the locations of NPCs, game entry points, and portals in Acts 2, 3, and 5.
As stated in the <b>Disclaimer</b> below, this mod is incompatible with other mods that modify map files. Therefore, this mod applies its own efficient changes, and please note that these specific adjustments are applied globally and cannot be toggled individually.

---

### Act II

<img src="https://i.imgur.com/KKbjpel.png" />﻿
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/VkDFThq.png" />
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/21Y2gfL.png" />

We aim for efficient movement by relocating portals, the game entry point, and placing Greiz (for mercenary hiring) near the portal. Additionally, some players perform "vendor runs" to reset Drognan's inventory by repeatedly walking in and out of the city when the exit is at the 1 o'clock position. To make this possible for the 11 o'clock layout as well, I have adjusted Drognan's position specifically for that map.
